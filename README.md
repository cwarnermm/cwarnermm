### 🤖 The Agentic Documentation Philosophy
I believe we are witnessing a fundamental shift: the evolution from **Generative AI** (which writes) to **Agentic AI** (which acts). I am positioning myself at the helm of this shift as a **Content Orchestrator**.

* **Documentation as a Decision Engine:** Documentation isn’t where we store truth; it’s where we decide what truth is safe to operationalize.
* **The Last Human Check:** I am the final human safeguard between engineering reality and customer safety. AI requires human governance to decide what is safe to say.
* **Agentic Orchestration:** I leverage AI as a "team" to monitor repos and draft content, allowing the human writer to focus on systemic quality, taxonomy, and accountability.
